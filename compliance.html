Compliance & Standards

Trading discipline, counterparty screening, and operational safeguards are built into the way Rayenna Oil evaluates and manages opportunities.

Our Compliance Framework

Aligned with global commodity trading best practices

Rayenna Oil operates with the highest standards of compliance, ensuring transactions are assessed against applicable regulations and internal processes designed to promote responsible trading conduct.

KYC Procedures

Full Know Your Customer procedures are applied to all counterparties, including identity verification, ownership structure review, and business legitimacy assessment.

Sanctions Screening

Counterparties and transactions are screened against OFAC, EU, UN, and other relevant sanctions lists before commercial engagement proceeds.

Credit Assessment

Structured credit evaluation helps assess financial soundness, mitigate counterparty risk, and support commercially prudent decision-making.

HSE Standards

Health, Safety, and Environment principles are applied across logistics, transport coordination, and supply chain planning where relevant.

AML / Anti-Money Laundering

Strict anti-money laundering controls and transaction awareness help align our conduct with applicable financial regulations and responsible trading practice.

Important: All enquiries and counterparties are subject to internal KYC, sanctions screening, credit assessment, and HSE standards, consistent with global commodity trading practices.
