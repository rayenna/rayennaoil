About Rayenna Oil

A responsible trading division built to connect reliable producers and buyers through disciplined execution, strong relationships, and a compliance-first mindset.

Who We Are

Connecting global energy flows with integrity and dependability

Rayenna Oil is the oil trading division of Rayenna Energy Pvt. Ltd., established with a clear commitment to reliability, ethics, and dependability in the global energy marketplace. We work to connect trusted producers with serious customers through commercially sound and operationally disciplined trading relationships.

With a growing network across the Middle East, Africa, and Asia, we bring regional market understanding together with practical execution capability in crude oil, refined products, lubricants, and petrochemicals.

Our role is not only to facilitate supply, but to build confidence through careful screening, transparent communication, and a strong respect for compliance requirements that shape international commodity trading.

Our Values

Principles that shape every trading relationship

We are guided by a practical set of values that define how we work with suppliers, buyers, and logistics partners.

Reliability

We deliver on our promises consistently and work to keep every commitment aligned with realistic commercial execution.

Ethics

We conduct business with integrity, transparency, and respect for legal and regulatory expectations across markets.

Dependability

Our counterparties can count on responsive communication, careful process management, and disciplined follow-through.

Excellence

We pursue continuous improvement in market knowledge, partner selection, documentation quality, and supply chain execution.

Vision & Mission

Focused on long-term trust and responsible growth

Vision

To be the most trusted and reliable energy trading partner in the Middle East, Africa, and Asia, recognized for integrity, expertise, and commitment to excellence.

Mission

To connect reliable producers with customers through safe, efficient, and compliant supply chains, delivering value to stakeholders while maintaining the highest standards of ethical conduct and operational excellence.
